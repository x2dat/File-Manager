### App Informations:
<br>
- Add Files: Easily attach local documents to your daily log. <br>
- Add Links: Save URLs for quick access later. <br>
- Create Notes: Write down thoughts or reminders directly in the app. <br>
- Modify Entries: Rename files or links and edit note content at any time. <br>
- Open Links: One click opens links in your system’s default browser. <br>
- Open Files: Launch documents directly through the app using native system handlers. <br>
- View Notes: Pop-up interface allows you to read or edit your notes without leaving the main view. <br>
- Chronological Storage: Everything is organized by date headers (e.g., 2026-04-08), keeping your history structured and searchable. <br>
- Local Storage: Data is stored 100% on YOUR device in a file_data.json file. No cloud, no tracking, total privacy. <br>
- Lightweight Build: Designed to be extremely light on system resources, even with large datasets. <br>
- No-Nonsense Design: A clean, intuitive interface that focuses on speed and utility. <br>
